Entry Requirements

Ordinary Diploma

Clinical Medicine

Holders of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with four (4) Passes in non-religious Subjects including "D" Passes in Chemistry, Biology and Physics/Engineering Sciences. A Pass in Basic Mathematics and English Language is an added advantage.

Ordinary Diploma

Nursing and Midwifery

Holders of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with four (4) Passes in non-religious Subjects including "D" Passes in Chemistry, Biology and Physics/Engineering Sciences. A Pass in Basic Mathematics and English Language is an added advantage.

Diploma

Community Development

Holder of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with four passes "D" in non-religious subjects AND Holders of Any Basic Technician Certificate (NTA Level 4) from NACTE recognized Institution OR Holder of Advanced Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (ACSEE) with at least one principle pass and two subsidiary passes in relevant subjects.
