Academic Programs Offered

Explore our diploma courses and their corresponding entry guidelines

Ordinary Diploma

Clinical Medicine

Entry Requirements

Holders of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with four (4) Passes in non-religious Subjects including "D" Passes in Chemistry, Biology, and Physics/Engineering Sciences.

Added Advantage: A Pass in Basic Mathematics and English Language.
Ordinary Diploma

Nursing and Midwifery

Entry Requirements

Holders of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with four (4) Passes in non-religious Subjects including "D" Passes in Chemistry, Biology, and Physics/Engineering Sciences.

Added Advantage: A Pass in Basic Mathematics and English Language.
Diploma

Community Development

Entry Requirements

Holder of Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with four passes "D" in non-religious subjects AND Holders of Any Basic Technician Certificate (NTA Level 4) from NACTE recognized Institution.

OR

Holder of Advanced Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (ACSEE) with at least one principle pass and two subsidiary passes in relevant subjects.
