Background Information

Established in 2013, the St. Augustine Institute of Health and Allied Sciences (SAMIHAS) traces its foundational roots back to the St. Anne Nurses Training School, founded in 1939 at Magila, Muheza. Decades of educational heritage have evolved into a premier modern institute offering elite training pathways in both Nursing and Clinical Medicine.

NACTVET Registered TNMC Accredited Clinical Partner: Muheza DDH

Our Vision

To become a recognized center of excellence for training healthcare professionals, executing quality clinical and health systems research, and delivering superior public health services.

Our Mission

To systematically pursue better community health profiles through transformative education, rigorous practical training, advanced research, and dedicated health services.

Our Core Values

The four core pillars of character and service at SAMIHAS.
