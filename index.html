Why Choose Us

St. Augustine Institute of Health and Allied Sciences was established in 2013 as an FBO Health Training Institution (HTI) fully registered by the National Council for Technical Education (NACTVET) with registration number REG/HAS/088.

It was established in 2013 for the purpose of improving the human resources for health through quality education in Health and Allied Sciences.

The institute is registered by NACTE and TNMC and offers training in Nursing, Clinical Medicine and community development in Tanzania

St. Augustine Institute of Health and Allied Sciences is located at Tanganyika Village along the teule road in Muheza District –Tanga region.
